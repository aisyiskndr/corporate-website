Aroma Lux

HISTORY

Aroma Lux was established on 21st October 2023 and sells pocket sized perfume as the main activity. Aroma Lux is a perfume business that is run by Aisy Iskandar, Fatin Nurulainnie, Mifzal Darwish and Nurul Ayu Hanani. Our business act as a retailer that sells perfume from a brand named “SKUSH”. We have chosen this brand as it provides products that suit our business objectives and could serve wide range of demographic and consequentially increase the ability to attract consumers especially students. The product consists of affordable pocket-sized perfumes with variety of scent for both genders.

OBJECTIVE
  • To give a pleasant and desirable scent to a person's body
  • To provide the best quality service to users
  • To provide affordable perfume with pocket size
  • To enhance health and well-being by improving mood
